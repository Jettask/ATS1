inside information & The Current Situation

Inside information 

The current situation up to now is that we have three locations. 

This year we acquired one additional company in Bradford with approximately 120 employees out of which 80 are working in production and 40 employees are in management and administration.

 The main location of the company remains in Bath with approximately 420 employees out of which 70 are employed in management, administration, marketing, sales and R&D.

 Next to those there are two more locations, one in Bolton with approximately 200 employees with 40 in management and administration and a smaller branch in Norwich specialized for custom made products. With approximately 60 employees out of which 43 are working in production and 17 are employed in management, administration and R&D.

 All branch offices use local providers of IT services. 

In the last two years ATS has moved toward the hybrid workspace, especially for R&D and HR departments. The majority of employees from management and administration departments during the last 2 years found themselves working from home. 

To cover the needs of remote and office workers during the pandemic we integrated MS Office 365. We started using MS Teams for meetings with great success. 

Due to this success, we have moved the majority of our support business to Microsoft Azure. 

Due to our exposure to industrial espionage and theft of intellectual property, we are seriously concerned about security beyond the usual level.

Therefore, we are interested in any solution that would increase our security and alleviate our consern. We are looking for a solution to handle sensitive electronic documents between sites, such as:  invoices, plans, designs and HR documents.

 We are also in need of a more reliable central solution for printer/PC management. We have a typical MPS office use model, (click on printer/pc estate for more information).

 Having paid for devices we didn't use during the pandemic we don't wish to have this problem again e.g MPS,desktops, etc.

We are looking for lessons learned from covid to what we can to digitalize.
